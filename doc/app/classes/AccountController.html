login()

If authorized (meaning they are an admin) then after a sucessful log in they are redirected to the admin index. If the logged in user is admitted then they are redirected to the student index. If neither, but still logged in successfully (meaning the account has been created) then they are sent to the thank you page, as the administrator has not yet admitted them.
